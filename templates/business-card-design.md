## Technical Specifications

### Print Requirements

| Specification | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| **Size** | 85x55mm (standard business card) |
| **Bleed** | 3mm all sides (final: 91x61mm) |
| **Safe Area** | 79x49mm (3mm from edge) |
| **Resolution** | 300 DPI minimum |
| **Color Mode** | CMYK for print |
| **File Format** | PDF (print-ready) |

### Color Palette

**Until brand colors defined:**
- **Primary Text:** CMYK 0/0/0/100 (Black)
- **Secondary Text:** CMYK 0/0/0/60 (Dark Gray)
- **Accent:** CMYK 0/60/100/0 (Placeholder - replace with brand color)
- **Background:** CMYK 0/0/0/0 (White)

### Typography

**Fonts (professional, widely available):**
- **Primary:** Inter, Open Sans, or Roboto
- **Name:** Bold weight
- **Title/Contact:** Regular weight
- **Hierarchy:** Clear size differentiation

## Design Guidelines

### Front Side
- **Focus:** Name and contact information
- **Logo:** Prominent but not overwhelming
- **Whitespace:** Generous margins for clean look
- **Readability:** High contrast, legible font sizes

### Back Side
- **Focus:** Company value proposition
- **QR Code:** Quick access to website/portfolio
- **Branding:** Consistent with front side
- **Function:** Memorable tagline, easy website access

---

## Production Notes

### Recommended Print Specs
- **Paper Stock:** 350gsm coated cardstock
- **Finish:** Matte or soft-touch laminate
- **Quantity:** Start with 250 cards
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 business days

### Print Checklist
- [ ] All text converted to outlines (or fonts embedded)
- [ ] 3mm bleed on all sides
- [ ] All elements within safe area
- [ ] CMYK color mode
- [ ] 300 DPI resolution
- [ ] PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4 format

## Why Roosevelt?

### From Employee to Entrepreneur

**November 2024:** After years at larger organizations, Sam founded Roosevelt to:

1. **Direct Client Relationships**
   - Work directly with clients instead of through employer
   - Build long-term partnerships
   - Maintain full ownership of solutions

2. **Independence & Control**
   - Choose projects that align with expertise
   - Set quality standards without compromise
   - Build own products alongside client work

3. **Strategic + Tactical Integration**
   - Eliminate handoff between strategist and developer
   - Ensure architectural vision survives implementation
   - Deliver cohesive solutions, not fragmented pieces

## Philosophy & Approach

### Work Principles

**Architecture First**
Foundation matters. Solid architecture prevents technical debt and enables scalability.

**Quality Over Speed**
Done right beats done fast. Shortcuts create maintenance nightmares.

**Strategy + Execution**
Ideas without implementation are dreams. Implementation without strategy is chaos.

**Direct Communication**
Transparency about progress, risks, and trade-offs. No surprises.

**Continuous Learning**
Technology evolves. Stay current or become obsolete.

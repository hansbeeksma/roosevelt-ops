## Email Best Practices

### General Guidelines
- **Subject Lines:** Clear, specific, max 50 characters
- **Length:** Keep under 200 words for first contact
- **Tone:** Professional but approachable
- **Call to Action:** One clear next step
- **Response Time:** Reply within 24 hours

### Formatting
- **Short Paragraphs:** Max 3-4 lines
- **Bullet Points:** For lists and clarity
- **Bold:** For emphasis on key points
- **White Space:** Generous spacing for readability

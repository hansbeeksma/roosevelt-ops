# Roosevelt OPS Design Strategy

> Vision, Positioning, Design Direction

---

## Design Vision

Roosevelt's digitale aanwezigheid moet drie dingen communiceren:
1. **Strategische diepgang** — We denken op architectuur-niveau
2. **Hands-on execution** — We bouwen zelf, geen handoff
3. **Kwaliteit** — Elk detail is bewust

---

## Positioning

| Against | Roosevelt Is | They Are |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Big consultancies | Wendbaar, direct contact, hands-on | Bureaucratisch, duur, PowerPoints |
| Freelancers | Strategisch, architectureel | Tactisch, beperkt overzicht |
| Agencies | One person: strategy to code | Handoffs, layers, overhead |
